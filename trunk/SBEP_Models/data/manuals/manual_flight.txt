Flying>T<(Reload) to enable mouseflight

Mouseflight Enabled: W and S are vertical ascent/descent.

Mouseflight Disabled: W and S are pitch up and pitch down.

In both cases,  A and D are bank left and bank right.

Alt to decelerate.

(Sprint) to accelerate.

Ctrl to toggle cockpit and exterior view
bind sbep_zoom_in and sbep_zoom_out to change the zoom levels
example: 'bind MWHEELDOWN "invnext; sbep_zoom_out 10"'
If you don't give the zoom a number it will default to 10

For Weapons, see "Weapons Systems."
